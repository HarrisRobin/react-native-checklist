### Generate App Icon & Splash-screen Manually {#generate-app-icon--splash-screen-manually}

Of course, you can still do all of this manually.



# iOS



1. Set `AppIcon` in `Images.xcassets`
2. Add 9 different size icons:

* 29pt
* 29pt\*2
* 29pt\*3
* 40pt\*2
* 40pt\*3
* 57pt
* 57pt\*2
* 60pt\*2
* 60pt\*3.
